    Frequently asked questions

    Why use Coach-Halo instead of finding a coach myself?

    You certainly can search for a coach on your own.

    Coach-Halo saves you the time and guesswork of sorting through dozens of profiles, websites, and coaching styles. We learn about your situation first, then introduce you to a coach who is likely to be a strong fit from the start.

    Is Coach-Halo matching really free?

    Yes. Submitting the form and receiving a coach introduction is completely free.

    We review your goals and personally introduce you to a coach who fits what you're looking for. Your first conversation is complimentary, so you can see if it's the right fit before making any commitment.

    How long does it take to get matched?

    Most people receive a coach introduction within seconds.

    Every submission is reviewed by a real person, not an automated system, so we can make thoughtful matches based on your goals, preferences, and timeline.

    What happens during the first conversation?

    Think of it as an introduction, not a sales call.

    You'll have a chance to discuss your goals, learn about the coach's approach, ask questions, and decide whether you'd like to work together. There is no obligation to continue.

    How do you choose my coach?

    We consider what you'd like help with, your goals, preferred coaching style, timeline, and coach availability.

    Our goal isn't to send you a random coach. It's to introduce you to someone who is genuinely well-positioned to help.

    What types of coaching are available?

    Our network includes coaches specializing in career transitions, job search, leadership, life coaching, relationships, confidence and mindset, health and wellness, and family coaching.

    Not sure what kind of coaching you need? That's perfectly okay. We'll help point you in the right direction.
